Information about Tanya from Mortal Kombat 1

Character Name: Tanya

Biography:Tanya is a character in the Mortal Kombat fighting game series. First presented as a treacherous and deceiving Edenian, Tanya has typically served as a minor antagonist in the series, siding with many of the major villains in the series. She serves as a major ally to the half-Tarkatan Mileena. Tanya made her playable debut in Mortal Kombat 4 and appeared in a few games afterwards, until being one of the many characters in Mortal Kombat: Armageddon. Tanya returned in Mortal Kombat X as a non-playable, albeit fightable character while also serving as a minor antagonist in the game's Story Mode. She later appeared as a DLC character, being the second available through DLC as part of the Kombat Pack. Tanya later returns playable again in Mortal Kombat 1.

Combo-Attack:

  • Air Fire Blast
  • Surging Blast

Special Moves:
