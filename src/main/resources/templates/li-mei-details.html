Information about Li-Mei from Mortal Kombat 1

Character Name: Li Mei

Biography: Li Mei is a character in the Mortal Kombat fighting game series. She is a warrior born from the village of Sun Do in Outworld. Li Mei made her debut in Mortal Kombat: Deadly Alliance. Li Mei returns as a playable fighter for the first time in Mortal Kombat 1, since her minor role in Mortal Kombat X and being playable for the first time since Mortal Kombat: Armageddon.

Combo-Attack:

  • Nova Blast
  • Flipping Heel Kick
  • Flying Flurry

Special Moves:

  • Lion's Pounce
  • Back Stomp
  • Half Circle
