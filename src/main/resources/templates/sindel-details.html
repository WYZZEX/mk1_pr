Information about Sindel from Mortal Kombat 1

Character Name: Scorpion

Biography: Sindel is a character in the Mortal Kombat fighting game series. She made her debut in Mortal Kombat 3. Sindel returned in Mortal Kombat 11 as a DLC character, being the fourth character available in the Kombat Pack. She also served as a secondary antagonist in the Aftermath Story Mode expansion, serving it alongside Shao Kahn. Sindel returns as a playable character in Mortal Kombat 1 while also serving as a supporting ally in the game’s story mode.

Combo-Attack:

  • Banshee Scream
  • Shriek
  • Levitate

Special Moves:

  • Mane Squeeze
  • Star Screamer
  • Step Up
