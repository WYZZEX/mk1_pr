Information about Smoke from Mortal Kombat 1

Character Name: Smoke

Biography:Tomas Vrbada, better known as Smoke, is a Lin Kuei assassin in the Mortal Kombat fighting game series. Smoke made his debut in Mortal Kombat II as an unplayable secret character, appearing as a human before making his playable debut in Mortal Kombat 3 as a cyborg. Smoke returned in Mortal Kombat (2011) as a playable character, appearing in his human form for the first time in 6 years. In Mortal Kombat X where he is a Revenant and a non-playable character, Tomas renounced the Smoke moniker in favor of calling himself Enenra. In Mortal Kombat 1, he returns to the title of Smoke.

Combo-Attack:

  • Teleport Punch
  • Smokeycut
  • Invisibility

Special Moves:

  • Stinky Fingers
  • Play Dead
  • Smoke Cloud
