Information about Ashrah from Mortal Kombat 1

Character Name: Ashrah

Ashrah is a character in the Mortal Kombat fighting game series, who made her debut in Mortal Kombat: Deception. She is a demoness who desires absolution from the Netherrealm, by slaying demons with an enchanted kriss sword called Datusha. Ashrah returns playable again for the first time in Mortal Kombat 1.

Combo-Attack:

  • Lightning Blast
  • Spin Cycle
  • Nature's Torpedo

Special Moves:

  • Demon's Wrath
  • Dark Ascension
  • Soul Slice
