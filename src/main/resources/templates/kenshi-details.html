Information about Kung-Lao from Mortal Kombat 1

Character Name: Kung-Lao

Biography: Kenshi Takahashi (高橋剣士) is a character in the Mortal Kombat fighting game series. A blind swordsman with the abilities of telekinesis & psychokinesis, he is the father of Takeda Takahashi and a major member of the Special Forces and the OIA. Kenshi made his playable debut in Mortal Kombat: Deadly Alliance. Kenshi returned in Mortal Kombat (2011) as DLC as part of the Season Pass. Kenshi returned in Mortal Kombat X as a playable character and as a supporting character in the game's Story Mode.

Combo-Attack:

  • Spear Special
  • Teleport Punch
  • Take Down

Special Moves:

  • Flame Breath
  • Hellfire
  • Death Spin
