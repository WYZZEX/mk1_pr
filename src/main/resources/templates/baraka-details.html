Information about Baraka from Mortal Kombat 1

Character Name: Baraka

Biography: Baraka is a character in the Mortal Kombat fighting game series. A general of the Tarkatan Horde, he has served as an antagonist throughout the series, serving Outworld and its Empire while being loyal to the half-Tarkatan Mileena and her father Shao Kahn. Baraka made his debut in Mortal Kombat II as the first playable Tarkatan of the series and has been a recurring character since. Baraka returned in Mortal Kombat 11 as a playable character while also serving as a supporting character in the game's Story Mode. A new version of Baraka appears in Mortal Kombat 1 as a playable character while once again serving as a supporting character in the game's Story Mode.

Special Moves:
